# 🛒 Supermarket Sales Analysis (Power BI)

## 📌 Overview

This project analyzes supermarket sales data to understand business performance and support decision-making for opening a new branch.

---

## 🎯 Objective

To find useful insights from sales data and help the company make better business decisions.

---

## 🛠️ Tools

* Power BI
* Excel

---

## 📊 Features

* Total sales, quantity, and rating
* Sales by city and branch
* Product and payment analysis
* Customer insights

---

## 🔍 Key Insights

* Mumbai has the highest sales
* Food & Beverages sell the most
* Male customers contribute more revenue
* Cash is the most used payment method

---

## 💡 Recommendations

* Open new branch in Mumbai
* Improve performance in low-sales cities
* Focus on top-selling products
* Encourage digital payments
